### Expected Review Times
- **Initial Review:** 24-48 hours (Medical category may take longer)
- **Update Review:** 12-24 hours

### If Rejected
1. Read rejection reason carefully
2. Respond to reviewer in Resolution Center
3. Make required changes
4. Resubmit with detailed notes addressing concerns

### Common Rejection Reasons to Avoid
