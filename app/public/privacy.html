Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Forge Partners Inc. ("we", "us" or "our") operates the PriorAuth Buddy mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using PriorAuth Buddy, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Important Privacy Commitment

Your health information stays on your device. We do not store any Protected Health Information (PHI) or Personally Identifiable Information (PII) related to your insurance cases on our servers. All case data including denial details, appeal letters and related information is stored locally on your device only.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Subscription status

2.2 Anonymous Analytics

We collect anonymized data to improve our services:

  • Appeal outcomes (win/loss)
  • Insurance company names
  • Procedure or medication types
  • General usage patterns

This data cannot be linked to your identity or specific case details.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash logs
  • Anonymous device identifiers

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect or store on our servers:

  • Your name, address or date of birth
  • Insurance policy numbers or member IDs
  • Medical diagnoses or procedure codes
  • Doctor names or medical facility information
  • The content of your appeal letters
  • Call recordings or transcripts from coaching sessions

All of this information remains stored locally on your device.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Authenticate your account
  • Process your subscription through Apple
  • Improve our AI suggestions based on anonymized outcomes
  • Fix bugs and improve App performance
  • Communicate with you about your account

5. AI Services and Data Processing

PriorAuth Buddy uses Anthropic Claude to power our AI features including appeal letter generation, denial analysis and call coaching.

AI Processing: When you use AI features, your inputs are sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic does not train its models on data sent through our API. All data transmission occurs over encrypted connections. We recommend you do not include your full name, specific member IDs or other sensitive identifiers in AI prompts.

AI-generated content is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical or legal advice. See our Terms of Service for full disclaimers.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

6.1 Supabase

We use Supabase for user authentication and account management. Your email and encrypted password are stored securely with Supabase. Supabase Privacy Policy

6.2 Anthropic

We use Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered features. Anthropic Privacy Policy

6.3 Apple

Subscription payments and billing are handled by Apple through App Store. Apple Privacy Policy

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All server communications use TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Our servers do not store PHI or case-specific details
  • Regular security reviews and updates

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

PriorAuth Buddy is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@priorauthbuddy.app.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

9.1 California Residents (CalOPPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is being collected
  • Know whether personal information is disclosed to third parties
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@priorauthbuddy.app.

9.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. This will remove your email and subscription information from our servers. Your locally stored case data can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

10. Data Retention

We retain your account information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. When you delete your account, your email and subscription data are removed from our servers within 30 days.

Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked to any individual user.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the effective date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
